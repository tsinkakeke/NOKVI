🧶 Beginner Terms

Here are some terms for beginners 💜

🪡 Stitch

A single loop used in crochet or knitting.

🧵 Yarn

The thread/material used for crochet and knitting.

✨ Pattern

A guide or instructions for making something.

🧶 Hook

The tool used for crochet.

🥢 Needles

The tools used for knitting.

OVERAL

First, you have to get needles/hooks and yarn and get to know terms for patterns, such as ch, sl st, sc, hdc, dc for crocheting and k, p, co, bo, k2tog for knitting.
